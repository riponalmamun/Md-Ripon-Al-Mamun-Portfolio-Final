Machine Learning and Explainable AI to Predict Heart Attacks

By Md Ripon Al MamunMar 8,2022

Energy-Efficient Hyperparameter Tuning Using Multi-Information Source Optimization and Augmented Gaussian Processes with Random Forest

I will be presenting a project titled "Energy-Efficient Hyperparameter Tuning Using Multi-Information Source Optimization and Augmented Gaussian Processes with Random Forest" at a conference in February 2025 in Dhaka, Bangladesh. This research introduces a novel hyperparameter tuning method that combines Multi-Information Source Optimization with Augmented Gaussian Process (MISO-AGP) and Random Forest (RF). Our approach enhances computational efficiency, reducing resource consumption and achieving energy savings of up to 70%, providing an efficient solution for optimizing machine learning models while balancing performance and energy usage.
